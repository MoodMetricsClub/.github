# MoodMetrics Organization

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for **MoodMetrics** — a cutting-edge mood and productivity tracking app designed to help knowledge workers, freelancers, and remote teams optimize their mental wellness and productivity through data-driven insights.

---

## About MoodMetrics

MoodMetrics is focused on providing users with a seamless experience to track daily moods, stress levels, and productivity patterns. Leveraging innovative AI-driven insights and personalized micro-interventions, MoodMetrics empowers users to improve focus, reduce stress, and maintain balanced well-being.

The MoodMetrics app integrates modern productivity features, mood tracking, and wellness content in one unified platform with a strong emphasis on privacy, security, and ease of use.

---

## Our Vision

To be the most trusted and user-friendly productivity and wellness companion that bridges mental health and work efficiency through smart data analytics and habit-forming product design.

---

## Key Features

- **Mood Tracking:** Intuitive daily mood logging with customizable emojis, scales, and contextual notes.
- **Stress Level Monitoring:** User-input and sensor integrations to measure and track stress.
- **Productivity Analytics:** Goal setting, Pomodoro timers, focus session tracking, and task management.
- **Insights & Visualizations:** Trends, heatmaps, and AI-powered correlations between mood, stress, and user productivity.
- **Wellness Content:** Curated micro-interventions like breathing exercises, motivational tips, and behavior nudges based on mood dips.
- **Privacy & Security:** GDPR and HIPAA compliance, encrypted local data storage, and secure cloud sync.
- **Notifications & Reminders:** Smart, customizable nudges for regular check-ins and wellness tasks.
